Creamy Chorizo Pasta

with Kale and Parmesan

Time: 30min
Portions: 2

We know everyone's looking for even better value when it comes to cooking these days - enter our Savour & Save recipes! These all cost $1 less per portion compared to recipes from our core menu and offer a simpler dish style, which still has all that Marley Spoon deliciousness you know and love.

Ingredients

What we send

  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 100g kale
  • 1 chorizo sausage
  • 5g Tuscan seasoning
  • 250ml cream
  • penne pasta
  • 25g parmesan

What you'll require

  • 1 tsp olive oil

Utensils

  • medium saucepan
  • medium deep frypan
  • slotted spoon
  • fine grater

Allergens

Gluten (1), Milk (2), Sulphites (3), Wheat (4). May contain traces of other allergens.

Nutrition per serving

Energy 1065kcal, Fat 66.1g, Carbs 85.0g, Protein 29.1g

Instructions

  1. Prep ingredients

    Bring a medium saucepan of salted water to the boil for the pasta. Finely chop the onion and tomatoes. Crush or finely chop the garlic. Discard the tough stems from the kale and thinly slice the leaves. Finely chop the chorizo.

  2. Cook chorizo

    Put the chorizo in a cold medium deep frypan over medium-high heat and cook, stirring, for 5-6 mins until golden and crisp. Remove from the pan with a slotted spoon, leaving the flavoured oil in the pan. Cover to keep warm.

  3. Simmer sauce

    Heat 1 tsp olive oil in the same pan over medium heat. Cook the onion and tomato, stirring, for 6-7 mins until soft. Add the garlic and Tuscan seasoning and cook, stirring, for 1 min or until fragrant. Stir in the cream and cook, stirring occasionally, for 3 mins or until slightly reduced. Taste, then season with salt and pepper.

  4. Cook pasta and kale

    Meanwhile, cook the pasta in the pan of boiling water for 6 mins. Add the kale and cook for a further 4 mins or until the pasta is al dente and the kale is tender. Reserve 2 tbs cooking water, then drain the pasta and kale.

  5. Grate parmesan

    While the pasta is cooking, finely grate the parmesan.

  6. Get ready to serve

    Add the pasta and kale to the sauce. Add the reserved cooking water and stir constantly until the sauce coats the pasta. Taste, then season with salt and pepper. Divide the pasta among bowls and scatter over the chorizo and parmesan to serve.
